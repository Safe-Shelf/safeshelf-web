1. Introduction

SafeShelf (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates a mobile-first product safety service that helps households scan products, maintain a watchlist, and receive safety-related alerts.

This Privacy Policy explains how [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] (“SafeShelf”) collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use the SafeShelf app, website, and related services (the “Service”).

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

For questions, contact us at support@safeshelf.app.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in three broad categories: information you provide, information collected automatically, and information from third parties.

2.1 Information you provide

Category Examples Why we collect it
Account information Email address, display name Create and secure your account; communicate with you
Authentication data Magic-link tokens (processed transiently) Verify your identity without passwords
Watchlist & product data Barcodes, product names, brands, categories, images, expiration dates you enter, notes Provide watchlist, alerts, and safety features
Consent records Timestamp of terms acceptance, terms version accepted Demonstrate compliance and control access to features
Support communications Messages you send to support Respond to your requests
Payment information Billing status, subscription tier, transaction references from app stores or payment processors Manage subscriptions (we do not store full payment card numbers)

2.2 Information collected automatically

Category Examples Why we collect it
Device & app data Device type, operating system, app version, language, time zone Operate, secure, and improve the Service
Usage data Feature interactions, scan events, alert opens, crash logs Understand usage, fix bugs, improve performance
Identifiers App instance identifiers, session tokens Keep you signed in and protect your account
Diagnostics Error reports (for example, via Sentry or similar tools) Monitor reliability and security

We aim to minimize collection to what is needed for the Service.

2.3 Information from third parties

Source Examples Why we collect it
Product & safety databases Public recall data, regulatory notices, catalog metadata tied to barcodes Display product and safety information
Authentication & payment partners App Store / Play Store receipt validation, Stripe customer IDs Confirm subscription status
AI service providers Prompts and context needed to generate summaries (see §5) Produce safety summaries and descriptions

We do not receive your full email content from your mail provider when you use magic-link sign-in; we only process the link/token you use to verify your identity.

2.4 Guest and pre-sign-in activity

If you scan a product before creating an account, we may temporarily store a pending barcode associated with your session so it can be claimed to your watchlist after you sign in. This data is linked to your account once you authenticate or is discarded according to our retention practices.

3. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

  1. Provide the Service — authenticate you, maintain your watchlist, deliver alerts, and show product safety information;
  2. Personalize your experience — for example, show items relevant to your saved products and subscription tier;
  3. Process subscriptions — manage free and paid plans, limits (such as watchlist size), and billing status;
  4. Communicate with you — send sign-in links, service announcements, security notices, and support responses;
  5. Improve and secure the Service — debug issues, prevent fraud and abuse, and analyze aggregated usage trends;
  6. Comply with law — respond to lawful requests and enforce our Terms of Service.

3.1 Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the Service you requested;
  • Legitimate interests — securing and improving the Service, preventing abuse, and understanding aggregated usage, balanced against your rights;
  • Consent — where required (for example, optional marketing communications or non-essential cookies on web);
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose data by law.

4. What we do not do with your data

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their own advertising or marketing.
  • We do not use your watchlist to target third-party ads based on sensitive health inferences beyond operating the Service.
  • We do not provide medical diagnoses or clinical recommendations; see our Terms for important disclaimers.

5. Artificial intelligence

Some product descriptions, safety summaries, or alert text may be generated or enhanced using AI. When we use AI:

  • We send only the minimum context needed (for example, product metadata and public safety text), not your full account history unless required for a specific feature disclosed in the app;
  • Outputs are reviewed through automated and human safeguards where appropriate, but may contain errors—you should verify critical information against official sources and packaging;
  • We contractually require AI subprocessors to use data only to provide services to SafeShelf, not to train public models on your personal watchlist content unless we explicitly disclose and obtain consent for that use.

6. How we share information

We share personal information only in limited circumstances:

Recipient Purpose
Service providers Hosting, databases, email delivery, analytics, error monitoring, payment processing, and AI inference—under contracts that limit use to providing services to us
Safety data providers Retrieving public recall and product catalog information (generally we query them; we do not sell your data to them)
App store platforms Subscription and purchase validation
Legal and safety When required by law, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security
Business transfers In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice where required

We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably identify you for analytics and product improvement.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy:

Data type Typical retention
Account profile Until you delete your account, plus a short period for backup and legal compliance
Watchlist & alerts Until you remove items or delete your account
Consent records For the life of the account and as required to demonstrate compliance
Pending barcodes (guest) Until claimed after sign-in or for a limited period if unclaimed
Logs & diagnostics Rolling window (for example, 30–90 days) unless needed for security investigations
Billing records As required by tax and accounting laws

We may retain anonymized or aggregated data longer.

8. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for API and web traffic;
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive stored data where supported by our infrastructure;
  • Access controls limiting employee and contractor access to production data;
  • Monitoring for abuse and anomalous activity.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact support@safeshelf.app immediately.
